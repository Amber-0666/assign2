Description of Hometown I was born and have lived in Kuching, Sarawak, for my entire life. Kuching, the capital city of Sarawak on the island of Borneo, is known for its rich cultural diversity, blending Malay, Chinese, and indigenous communities like the Iban and Bidayuh. The city is famous for landmarks such as the Kuching Waterfront and its iconic cat statues, earning it the nickname 'Cat City.' It is also a food lover's paradise, offering dishes like vegetarian Sarawak Laksa (a spicy coconut broth with rice noodles, tofu, and fresh herbs) and Kolo Mee (springy egg noodles with mushrooms, carrots, and greens), reflecting its vibrant culinary culture.
